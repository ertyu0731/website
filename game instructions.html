Flappy Q&A - How to Play

Purpose of the Game

This game helps you learn about coding, cybersecurity, and leadership in a fun and interactive way.

How to Play

  • Click or press Space or Up Arrow to jump the bird.
  • Dodge obstacles (pipes) and keep flying.
  • If you hit an obstacle, you'll be asked a question.
  • Answer questions correctly to continue playing.
  • You can also choose to restart or answer a mini quiz to get a second chance.

Controls

Jump by clicking or pressing Space / Up Arrow.
