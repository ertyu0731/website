About Me

Hi! I’m Kmy Er Sze Lei, a 15‑year‑old student at the School of Science and Technology, Singapore. My twin passions are leadership and computer science. From running coding workshops for younger kids to building SwiftUI apps (Calorize, Xuemi, Spotify Unwrapped), I love turning ideas into tools that empower people.

Beyond the screen, I’ve had the privilege of representing my school overseas—#Project Serve in Vietnam and two Hack Club events in San Francisco—broadening my perspective on how tech can drive community impact.
